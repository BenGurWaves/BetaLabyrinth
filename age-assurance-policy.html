This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal information when you use Labyrinth™ (the “Service”). It applies to all users and is incorporated into our Terms and Conditions and Age Assurance Policy.

We are committed to transparency, data minimization (especially for minors), and child safety. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your private messages or Realm content to train artificial intelligence or machine learning models.

1. Notice at Collection (California CPRA and General Transparency)

By creating an account or using the Service, you acknowledge the data practices described below.

1.1 Categories of Personal Information We Collect, Purposes, and Sharing

Category Examples of Data Collected Purposes Shared With

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

1.2 Data Minimization for Users Under 18

For users who declare or are verified as under 18, we collect only what is necessary:

- Email, username, date of birth, hashed password, and limited IP/logs for security.
- We do *not* collect precise location, phone numbers, detailed device fingerprints, or unnecessary metadata.
- Traffic and interaction logs are retained for shorter periods (30 days) and used only for safety and security.

2. How We Use Personal Information

We use your information only to:

- Provide, operate, and improve the Service
- Authenticate and manage accounts
- Enforce age restrictions and safety rules
- Detect and prevent abuse, fraud, or security issues
- Comply with legal obligations (including child safety reporting)
- Analyze usage to maintain performance

We do *not* use private content for marketing, profiling, or AI training.

3. User Content and Ownership

3.1 You Own Your Content

You retain full ownership and all rights to the messages, posts, files, and other content you create.

3.2 Limited License

You grant us a revocable, non-exclusive, royalty-free license to store, display, transmit, and back up your content solely to operate the Service and enforce our policies.

3.3 No AI or Machine Learning Use

We do *not* use your private messages or Realm content to train AI/ML models (ours or third-party). This prohibition applies to us, our Service Providers, and any future acquirer.

4. Data Deletion and Retention

4.1 How to Request Deletion

Delete your account via in-app settings or by emailing labyrinthchat@gmail.com with subject "DELETION REQUEST."

4.2 Deletion Process and Timeline

- Day 0–2: Verification and immediate disable of account access.
- Day 2–14: Removal from active systems (profile, content, messages).
- Day 14–60: Deletion from backups and Service Provider systems.
- Day 60: Final confirmation email sent.

You will receive status updates and a final confirmation.

4.3 Limited Exceptions

- Hashed email/username retained up to 3 years for fraud prevention (non-reversible, not used for any other purpose).
- Content flagged for CSAM/abuse preserved until investigation resolves.
- Data subject to legal hold or law enforcement request retained as required.

4.4 Service Provider Coordination

We instruct Supabase, Cloudflare, and other providers to delete your data (including backups) per the above timeline.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

All providers are bound by Data Processing Agreements requiring security, limited use, and deletion on request.

If we use third-party age verification services, they will receive date of birth and (if needed) ID data solely for verification, which they delete promptly after use.

Data is primarily processed in the U.S. We use appropriate safeguards (including Standard Contractual Clauses) for international transfers.

6. Sensitive Personal Information and Minors

Data of users under 16 is treated as Sensitive Personal Information. It is used only for age assurance, access control, safety, and legal compliance. It is not sold, shared for marketing, or used for profiling.
